WORLD AND LORE

Underwood follows the breakdown of a large city colony that's grown too large to support itself. Rivalries once kept on a thin tether finally and at long last snap, plunging the group into civil conflict. Out of the dust, escapes our new cast! The roleplay begins with the city outcasts traveling into the wilderness, sparking tension with the resident forest Clan. There they'll be forced to acclimate to life in the wild, to form new loyalties or fail trying, and all while navigating the complex relationship with the forest dwellers.

SETTING

Set in North America, Underwood's geography is inspired by the Pacific Northwest and California coast, but is not wholly anchored in a specific location. Instead, it combines multiple landmarks and geographic features to provide a complex territory for the Clans to navigate.
